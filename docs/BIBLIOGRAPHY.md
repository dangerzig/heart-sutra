## Primary Sources

### Chinese Canon (Taishō Shinshū Daizōkyō)

**T223** — 摩訶般若波羅蜜經 (*Móhē bōrě bōluómì jīng*). Trans. Kumārajīva 鳩摩羅什, 404 CE. Taishō Vol. 8, No. 223. [Large Prajñāpāramitā Sūtra, source text for Heart Sūtra excerpts]

**T250** — 摩訶般若波羅蜜大明咒經 (*Móhē bōrě bōluómì dàmíng zhòu jīng*). Attr. Kumārajīva 鳩摩羅什, date disputed. Taishō Vol. 8, No. 250. [Short recension, "Great Clarity Mantra Sūtra"]

**T251** — 般若波羅蜜多心經 (*Bōrě bōluómìduō xīnjīng*). Trans. Xuanzang 玄奘, ca. 649–656 CE. Taishō Vol. 8, No. 251. [Short recension, base text for this edition]

**T252** — 般若波羅蜜多心經 (*Bōrě bōluómìduō xīnjīng*). Trans. Kumārajīva 鳩摩羅什 (attr.). Taishō Vol. 8, No. 252.

**T253** — 般若波羅蜜多心經 (*Bōrě bōluómìduō xīnjīng*). Trans. Fayue 法月, 738 CE. Taishō Vol. 8, No. 253.

**T254** — 普遍智藏般若波羅蜜多心經 (*Pǔbiàn zhìzàng bōrě bōluómìduō xīnjīng*). Trans. Prajñā 般若, 790 CE. Taishō Vol. 8, No. 254.

**T255** — 般若波羅蜜多心經 (*Bōrě bōluómìduō xīnjīng*). Trans. Prajñācakra 智慧輪, ca. 850 CE. Taishō Vol. 8, No. 255.

**T256** — 唐梵翻對字音般若波羅蜜多心經 (*Táng fàn fān duì zìyīn bōrě bōluómìduō xīnjīng*). 788 CE. Taishō Vol. 8, No. 256. [Bilingual transliteration]

**T257** — 佛說聖佛母般若波羅蜜多經 (*Fó shuō shèng fómǔ bōrě bōluómìduō jīng*). Trans. Dānapāla 施護, 1005 CE. Taishō Vol. 8, No. 257. [Long recension, translated from Sanskrit]

### Chinese Epigraphy

**Fangshan Stele** — 房山石經, 661 CE. Fangshan, Hebei Province. [Earliest securely dated Heart Sūtra witness]

### Dunhuang Manuscripts

**S.700** — Stein Collection 700. British Library. 7th–8th century. [T250-type]

**S.2464** — Stein Collection 2464. British Library. 7th–8th century. [T251-type]

**P.2168** — Pelliot Collection 2168. Bibliothèque nationale de France. 8th–9th century.

**P.2323** — Pelliot Collection 2323. Bibliothèque nationale de France. 851 CE (dated colophon).

**P.3917** — Pelliot Collection 3917. Bibliothèque nationale de France. 8th–9th century. [Bilingual]

### Sanskrit Manuscripts

**Ja (Hōryū-ji)** — Palm-leaf manuscript. Hōryū-ji Temple, Nara, Japan. 7th–8th century. [Earliest Sanskrit witness; short recension]

**Na** — Cambridge University Library Add. 1164. 11th century. Nepal.

**Nb** — Cambridge University Library Add. 1485. 1677 CE. Nepal. [Contains *oṃ* in mantra]

**Nc** — Royal Asiatic Society, London. 19th century. Nepal.

**Nd** — India Office Library. 18th–19th century. Nepal.

**Ne** — NGMPP (Nepal-German Manuscript Preservation Project). 15th–17th century. Nepal.

### Tibetan Canon (Kangyur)

**D (Degé)** — སྡེ་དགེ། Degé Kangyur, Toh 21. 1733 CE. [Standard reference edition]

**Q (Peking)** — པེ་ཅིན། Peking Kangyur. 1684–1692 CE.

**N (Narthang)** — སྣར་ཐང་། Narthang Kangyur. 1730–1732 CE.

**H (Lhasa)** — ལྷ་ས། Lhasa Kangyur. 1934 CE.

**J (Lithang)** — ལི་ཐང་། Lithang Kangyur. 1609–1621 CE. [Earliest printed Kangyur]

**S (Stog)** — སྟོག Stog Palace Manuscript. 14th–15th century. [Oldest Kangyur manuscript]

### Dunhuang Tibetan

**IOL Tib J 751** — India Office Library (now British Library), Stein Tibetan Collection. c. 823 CE. Short recension without frame narrative. Earliest securely datable Tibetan Heart Sūtra witness. Imperial-period orthography (dbu can script).

**84000** — Translating the Words of the Buddha. "The Heart of the Perfection of Wisdom." https://read.84000.co/

## Index of Textual Sections

| Section | Chinese (T251) | Content |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| I | 觀自在菩薩...照見五蘊皆空 | Avalokiteśvara's observation |
| II | 色不異空...受想行識亦復如是 | Form and emptiness equation |
| III | 是諸法空相...不增不減 | Characteristics of dharmas |
| IV | 是故空中無色...無意識界 | Negation of skandhas, āyatanas, dhātus |
| V | 無無明...無苦集滅道 | Negation of pratītyasamutpāda, Four Truths |
| VI | 無智亦無得...得阿耨多羅三藐三菩提 | Bodhisattva practice and Buddha attainment |
| VII | 故知般若波羅蜜多...真實不虛 | Praise of the mantra |
| Mantra | 揭帝揭帝... | *gate gate pāragate...* |
